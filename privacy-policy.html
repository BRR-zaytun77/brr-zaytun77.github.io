Privacy Policy for MemoPic

Last updated: January 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures on the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use the MemoPic mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

MemoPic is designed to provide a minimalist and effective language learning experience. To provide and improve the Service, we collect:

  • Device Identifiers: We use Apple's Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) and Google Advertising ID (AAID) to provide personalized advertisement experiences.
  • Device Information: Information about your mobile device (model, OS version) to ensure app stability.
  • Usage Data: Statistics on how you interact with flashcards to improve our learning algorithms.

2. Advertising and Tracking

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google AdMob: Our app displays advertisements. Google may use device identifiers to serve personalized ads based on your interests.
  • App Tracking Transparency (iOS): On iOS devices, we will request your permission to track your activity across other apps and websites for advertising purposes. You can opt-out at any time via device settings.

3. Firebase and Analytics

We use Google Firebase for the following purposes:

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): To send study reminders and updates.
  • Firebase Analytics: To understand how users interact with the app. Firebase automatically collects certain information, such as unique device IDs and usage patterns.

4. Local Data Storage

Your learning progress, custom flashcards, and language preferences (English, Russian, Uzbek, etc.) are stored locally on your device. This data is not linked to your personal identity in our current version as we do not require user registration.

5. Children's Privacy

MemoPic does not address anyone under the age of 4. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.
